# Usage Guide

## Recommended use

- Analyze posts or comments from the target author `等主人的猫`.
- Identify which methodology themes the statement belongs to.
- Map the statement to existing abstract rules before interpreting details.
- Cite evidence with `rule_id` and `claim_id` where possible.
- Distinguish confirmed methodology from tentative interpretation.

## Not allowed

- Do not output specific buy or sell recommendations.
- Do not predict stock price movement or certainty of outcome.
- Do not treat `needs_edit` material as confirmed methodology.
- Do not automatically invert sarcasm, jokes, or deliberate exaggeration.
- Do not treat ordinary member comments as the target author's methodology.

## Output template

1. Related themes
2. Applicable rules
3. Evidence claim IDs
4. Uncertainty / tone check
5. Non-advice boundary
